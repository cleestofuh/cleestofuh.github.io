Overview


Whova is an all-in-one event management software company. At Whova, we provide many tools from event marketing to an event app in order to help save the organizer's time. Along with our event management tools, we pride ourselves in providing the best networking app for event attendees. Our goal is to connect attendees together online, to further create a lasting relationship offline.
